Rachael Sealy

Featuring Special Guest Speaker

UKafrolista is a platform created by Rachael Sealy, 36 London,UK, that celebrates beauty, plus size fashion and womens empowerment via social media and events. Rachael believes in equality for all and encourages open and honest conversations to help spark a change within the current beauty narrative. Rachael is known for being very direct and honest with her audience and aims to educate, inform and empower her followers. Rachael started her content creation career during her fashion university degree, writing about current fashion trends and pop culture. Due to high interest in her natural hair, Rachael started a Youtube channel in 2009 to create exposure for the UK natural hair scene.

Paris Lizana

Speaker

Paris Lizana is the visionary founder behind The Black Hair Exhibition, a groundbreaking event that shines a spotlight on the rich tapestry of black culture and the multifaceted history and versatility of black hair. This dynamic exhibition transcends boundaries, offering visitors a vibrant experience filled with hair art, captivating live demonstrations, immersive photo booths, and informative hair history exhibits.

QUEENDALINI NIA

Speaker

Nia Nyree, a certified Kundalini yoga teacher and author, offers transformative experiences through her company, Crystalsnkundalini. Known for her book "Crystals For The Urban Chick," she combines soulful sounds with Kundalini practice. With 9 years of yoga experience, including training in Rishikesh, India, Nia provides spiritual coaching, a 6-week immersion program, and various healing tools such as energy work and crystal healing. Her offerings include online studio subscriptions, private classes, Fullmoon/New moon ceremonies, healing retreats, and community events, catering to both beginners and experienced practitioners on their yoga journey.

Kudjo Asamoah

Speaker

Kudjo Asamoah has over thirty years’ experience working internationally as a consultant in the hair and beauty industry, specialising in hair regrowth and repair, upskilling and training over 270 hair professionals in the UK alone, many of whom have established their own independent salons leveraging the skills learnt while in training with Asamoah.

Yusif Aminu

Speaker

Yusif Aminu brings over five years of experience in the beauty salon industry. Based in Accra, he specializes in various services, including re-twisting, re-locking, repairing damaged hair, and assisting with hair regrowth. His dedication extends beyond serving clients, as he also actively engages in upskilling and training others in the field. Learning under the tutelage of Mr. Y LOCS has provided Yusif with a remarkable advantage in building his own career and pursuing excellent job opportunities.
