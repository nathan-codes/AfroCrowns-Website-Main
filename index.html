About Conference

Akosua and Associates is pleased to invite all enthusiasts and lovers of African hair and beauty to the AfroCrowns Hair and Beauty Festival conference, which will be held in Accra on Saturday, 9th March, 2024. This event also offers an optional tour package for non-Ghanaians visiting during March 2024. Ask us about tour availability.

AfroCrowns is a day event that aims to showcase the uniqueness of African Hair and Beauty by presenting the creative art of it. AfroCrowns is focusing on bridging the gap between Ghanaians and the international market for hair care and beauty products by introducing the first ever networking event.

The event comprises three interesting folds;

  • Hair Care Demonstration and Technique workshop
  • Sophisticated Guest speakers in the field of Natural Hair presenting on the unknown tips of growing natural hair during a short period
  • International Natural Hair product vendors selling the newest innovative hair care products Pointers
  • Post Event Business opportunities for distribution of Hair Care and Wellness Products.
  • Youth Activities; Double Dutch Exhibition
